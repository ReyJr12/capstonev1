College of Computer Studies


Community Outreach: Create, Connect, & Design

Target Group: AMA Basic Education of Las Piñas and other interested student Activity: Adobe Photoshop Workshop

The UPHSD College of Computer Studies (CCS) hosted a free graphic design class for Grade 12 ICT students from AMA Basic Education of Las Piñas as well as other interested students.

The graphic design workshop was held in Room 206 (Mac Laboratory) in the CCS Building. The program, which lasted from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., covered subjects varying from Adobe Photoshop to essential design ideas.

The session is hosted by CCS Professor Efren Semancio and President James Wendel Decipeda, with other presenters including Vice President Genel Grace Amihan, Secretary Loraine Mendoza, and Business Manager Rafael David Rodriguez.
